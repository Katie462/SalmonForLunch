Dill Salmon

  • Time: 45 minutes
  • Serves: 4 people (or 2.3 Giraffes)
Ingredients Quantity
Salmon 16 ounces
Olive Oil 3 tbsp
Dill enough to cover both
sides of each filet
Pepper Cracked to taste
